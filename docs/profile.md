# Profile

Your profile is used to link patches and comments to you.
Please take a minute to configure it!

## Profile Settings

### Setting Your Name

1. Click your **profile avatar** (top left)
2. Enter your name, and optionally an Email address and a profile picture
3. Your name appears on:
   - Comments you create
   - Document metadata
   - Timeline patches
4. Choose a colour to make it easy to quickly identify your edits
